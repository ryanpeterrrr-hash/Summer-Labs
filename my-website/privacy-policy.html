Privacy Policy

Version 1.0  ·  June 2026

1. Introduction

SummerLabs ("we", "us", "our") is an AI automation platform based in Perth, Western Australia. We provide businesses with AI-powered tools including voice agents, automated quoting, scheduling, CRM, and related services.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of individuals in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, hold, use and disclose personal information, and how you can contact us with any privacy concerns.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Kinds of Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name and details
  • Australian Business Number (ABN) or Australian Company Number (ACN)
  • Usage data and interaction patterns with our AI systems
  • Voice recordings and transcripts generated through our voice agent services
  • Behavioural patterns derived from your use of our platform and services

We only collect personal information that is necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Where possible, we will give you the option to interact with us anonymously or by pseudonym.

3. How We Collect Personal Information

We collect personal information through the following means:

  • Contact and booking forms on our website
  • Direct contact with us via email, phone or in person
  • Website usage tracking (pages visited, session duration, interactions)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 8 below)
  • Your use of our AI automation services and platform

We collect information directly from you wherever practicable. In some cases we may collect information from third parties such as technology partners or publicly available sources, where permitted by law.

4. Purpose of Collection and Use

We collect, hold and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and process service requests
  • To provide, operate and maintain our AI automation services
  • To improve and refine our AI models and platform
  • To personalise your experience and tailor our services to your business needs
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse or security incidents
  • To comply with applicable legal requirements and regulatory obligations
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, services or support

We will not use your personal information for a secondary purpose unless you have consented, or unless it is permitted or required by law.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform and delivering our services (for example, cloud infrastructure providers, form processing services). All such providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your information to perform the services we have engaged them for.

We will not disclose your personal information outside of Australia unless:

  • You have provided your consent, or
  • We are required or authorised to do so by Australian law

We may also disclose your information where required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property or safety of SummerLabs, our clients or others.

6. Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement the following safeguards:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Strict access controls limiting access to authorised personnel only
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with reputable providers
  • Regular security audits and reviews of our systems and processes

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Where Australian law requires retention of business records (including financial and transactional records), we retain relevant information for a minimum of 7 years.

When personal information is no longer required, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it securely.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — to understand website traffic and usage patterns (data is anonymised)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request correction of any inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete or misleading information.

To make an access or correction request, please contact us at contact@summerlabs.io. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. In some circumstances, we may be required or permitted by law to refuse access or correction, in which case we will explain our reasons.
